Instructions

Shoot all aliens to proceed to the next level. If you get hit by a bullet or the aliens reach the bottom, you die. Shields can absorb hits from bullets, but they will disintegrate. Shoot enemy bullets to destroy them. Shooting invaders will give you 10 points whilst shooting the ufo grants 50 points.
